About the Unit

We follow the ethos of The Bharat Scouts & Guides and the global Scouting movement. Our programme blends service, skills, and adventure for students aged 17–25 — building leadership, community awareness, and life skills.

  • Inclusive youth-led governance
  • Camps, treks, and pioneering
  • Community service & relief efforts
  • Awards pathway & personal development

Affiliated: The Bharat Scouts & Guides — Himachal Pradesh
